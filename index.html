About Us

We take the time to build it right.

We are a Mangalore based civil contracting firm delivering work on public, private and collaborative projects across the coastal region.

Rooted in the coast. Built on trust.

Kamala Constructions has been serving Dakshina Kannada since 2021. We take on direct contracts as well as specialist civil scope for established construction firms, which gives us a broad range of on-ground experience and a steady focus on execution.

We have delivered scope on Smart City walkways in Mangalore, Multi Village water supply pipelines across three taluks, sewage treatment plants and retaining walls at Kateel temple, foundation works at Kalasa, and a full residential renovation. Each job is handled with the same care, whether it is a 300 KLD STP or a community road.

We believe good work speaks for itself. No shortcuts. No tall claims. Just steady, thoughtful construction that holds up over time.
